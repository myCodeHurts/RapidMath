Hill Climbing Method

• Identify the dividend and divisor.
• Split divisor, dividend into two parts : upper & lower fields. Dividend lower field has same number of digits as the divisor lower field.

• Go forward with only 2 steps :
-Climb Down by Division, Subtraction
-Climb Up by Carrying over Remainder.

• Subtract, but do not divide in the final descent. This is the remainder of the division operation..
